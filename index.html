What are Autism Resources?

The Autism Resource Page provides families with autistic children with the ability to access their traditional resources in an online format. Cards such as Choice Boards, Stoplight, and First-Then Boards have been digitized, allowing far easier usage in a format oftentimes more comfortable for autistic children. These resources were developed by the TechHOUNDS Robotics Team in partnership with HANDS in Autism, an interdisciplinary autism support group based in Indianapolis.
